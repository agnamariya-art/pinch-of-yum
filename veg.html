Veg Recipes

Healthy and delicious vegetarian dishes

Vegetable Curry

Ingredients: Mixed vegetables, onions, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, curry powder, oil, salt.

Method: Sauté onions and garlic, add tomatoes, spices, and chopped vegetables. Simmer until vegetables are cooked. Serve with rice or bread.

Pasta Primavera

Ingredients: Pasta, bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper.

Method: Cook pasta, stir-fry vegetables in olive oil with garlic, mix pasta with veggies, season, and serve hot.
