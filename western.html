Western Food Recipes

Popular dishes from the West

Pizza Margherita

Ingredients: Pizza dough, tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, olive oil.

Method: Spread sauce on dough, top with cheese and basil, drizzle olive oil, bake at 220°C for 10–12 minutes.

Beef Burger

Ingredients: Beef patty, burger buns, lettuce, tomato, cheese, sauces.

Method: Grill patty, toast buns, assemble with vegetables, cheese, and sauces. Serve hot.
