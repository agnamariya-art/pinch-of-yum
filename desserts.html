Dessert Recipes

Sweet treats to make your day special

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients: Flour, cocoa powder, sugar, butter, eggs, milk, baking powder.

Method: Mix dry ingredients, then add wet ingredients. Pour into a greased tin, bake at 180°C for 35 minutes. Cool and serve.

Homemade Cookies

Ingredients: Flour, butter, sugar, eggs, vanilla essence, baking soda.

Method: Cream butter and sugar, add eggs and vanilla, mix in dry ingredients. Shape cookies, bake at 170°C for 12 minutes.
