Bakery Recipes

Freshly baked pastries and treats

Croissants

Ingredients: Flour, butter, yeast, milk, sugar, salt.

Method: Prepare dough, layer with butter, fold and roll several times, cut into triangles, roll into croissant shape, bake at 200°C for 20 minutes.

Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients: Flour, yeast, milk, sugar, cinnamon, butter.

Method: Prepare dough, roll out, spread with butter and cinnamon sugar, roll up, slice, let rise, bake at 180°C for 25 minutes. Glaze if desired.
